ENGAGE

A YouTube intelligence tracker for English-language Indian news. Built to answer not "who won YouTube this week" — but what kinds of journalism work on the platform, and why.

What this is

Engage is a competitive intelligence tool for news organisations and editorial strategy teams. It tracks 21 English-language Indian news channels — television, print-origin, and digital-native — and surfaces normalised, methodology-driven signals from their YouTube output.

Raw view counts answer the wrong question. Engage asks: how efficiently is each channel converting editorial effort into audience engagement? Which content formats hold attention beyond the 24-hour news cycle? Who is doing more with less?

Who it's for

Editors, digital heads, and strategy teams at news organisations who want to understand their competitive position on YouTube without wading through raw platform data.

What it is not

Engage does not rank channels by raw size. A television channel with millions of subscribers will not appear on the same leaderboard as a digital-native outlet with a fraction of that following. The three segments — Television, Print, Web-only — are kept separate throughout all analysis.

Built by

Engage is a portfolio project built at the intersection of data journalism, editorial judgment, and data engineering. The data pipeline runs daily on the YouTube Data API v3. Analysis is produced in Python. Visualisations are served via Datawrapper.

ENGAGE Data fetched daily via YouTube Data API v3. Videos enter analysis 24 hours after publication. Fetch window: 30 days prior to yesterday 12:00 IST. Content types broadcast_archive and live_or_premiere excluded from all charts.
