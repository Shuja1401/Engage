ENGAGE tracks the YouTube performance of leading Indian news publishers across television, print and digital-native brands. Updated daily, it monitors publishing activity, audience engagement and content performance to identify patterns shaping the news ecosystem. The platform is designed to help editors, journalists and media professionals understand how competitors are publishing, what audiences are consuming and where engagement is being generated. All metrics are calculated from publicly available YouTube data and are intended to provide a comparative view of the Indian news video landscape.

How Engaged Is the Audience?

The Engagement Score measures a channel's ability to generate audience interaction through likes and comments. To ensure a fair comparison, the score is normalised for views, allowing channels of different sizes to be evaluated on the same scale.

Engagement score = ((Likes × 3.0 + Comments × 1.0) ÷ Views) * 100

How Much Content Is Being Produced?

A channel's operational efforts are measured using a composite score, Editorial Effort. It captures two dimensions of a channel's content production activity: how many videos it publishes, and the total runtime of those videos.

This chart compares a channel's publishing volume with the average views generated per hour by its videos. Each coloured-dot represents a channel in a different group. Channels further to the right publish more videos, while channels higher on the chart generate more views per hour.
