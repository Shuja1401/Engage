Engagement

Engagement score

Engagement score measures the ability of a video to engage its audience using comments and likes.

Engagement score = (Likes × 3.0 + Comments × 1.0) ÷ Views
Score is for videos published in the previous 30 days.

Editorial effort

Shelf life

Shelf life of a video. This will be calculated only for videos with more than 1000 views and 30 days of data. The shelf life of a video is defined as the number of days taken by it to accumulate 80% of the total views in the last 30 days.
