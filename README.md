# ENGAGE

ENGAGE is a YouTube intelligence tracker built to help newsrooms observe how competing publishers produce and package digital content.

The system tracks the publishing behaviour of 20+ English-language Indian news channels and analyses patterns across publishing volume, video duration, content formats and audience engagement.

The broader objective is to move from:

> What is happening?

to:

> What decisions can be taken based on this information?

## What ENGAGE does

- Tracks YouTube publishing activity across news publishers
- Analyses publishing volume and content formats
- Monitors video duration and audience engagement
- Uses derived metrics to identify patterns in publishing behaviour
- Supports comparison of production behaviour across publishers

## Current direction

ENGAGE is being developed as a decision-support system.

The next layer focuses on generating recurring, decision-oriented insights from the data and exploring how parts of the insight-generation process can be automated.
